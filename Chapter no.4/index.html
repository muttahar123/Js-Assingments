VARIABLE NAMES: LEGAL & ILLEGAL

Legal Variable Names:

  1. myVar
  2. _count
  3. totalAmount
  4. first_name
  5. $price

Illegal Variable Names:

  1. 123var (starts with a digit)
  2. my-var (contains a hyphen)
  3. illegal variable (contains a space)
  4. #count (contains a special character other than underscore)
  5. for (uses a reserved keyword)
